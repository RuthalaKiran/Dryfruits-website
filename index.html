About Us

Dry fruits have been a part of our diet and culture since times immemorial. We, Wellbeing Dry Fruits located in Malad West, Mumbai, Maharashtra, provide roasted & Salted almond, cashew, pistachios & raisin of premium quality. Our carefully chosen nuts & dry fruits are roasted, flavoured & paked in a state-of-the-art manner. We believe in gathering the best of nature & making it affordable to our customers.
